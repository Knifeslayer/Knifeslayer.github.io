Welcome! You found my website — which means two things:

The domain name wasn’t already owned by a cryptocurrency startup, and
I managed to wire the DNS settings correctly without accidentally deleting the internet.

Both are miracles.

This is the official home of my writing, book updates, and whatever else I decide to put here when procrastinating on actual chapters. If something looks broken, it was definitely intentional and part of my “minimalist aesthetic” (or Cloudflare did it — we blame them).

Here’s what you can expect:

  • Updates on current book progress
  • Occasional behind-the-scenes notes from my writing cave
  • Honest admissions that I’m behind schedule
  • Maybe pictures of my cat (if I ever get one)
  • Definitely no inspirational productivity advice because I have none

In the meantime, feel free to look around, check out the books page, or send me a message through my shiny new author email — which hopefully forwards correctly and doesn’t disappear into a cosmic void.

Thanks for being here.
It means a lot — and it makes all the late nights worth it.
